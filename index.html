Tell the Next President to Stand With Refugees

In the wake of the terrorist attacks in Brussels, politicians across the country are saying hateful things about refugees. We won't allow this to continue. Sign the open letter to our presidential candidates and ask them to stand for true American values in the face of terror.

To the Presidential Candidates:

I am deeply disturbed by some of the recent comments that have been made about refugees in the wake of the terrorist attacks in Brussels. Refugee families are fleeing for their lives, trying to escape the very same kind of violence that tragically struck Brussels. America should support these families in their search for freedom.

Fear and hatred are not American values. It is in our country's interest to have strong relationships with communities around the world.

I urge you to show that you stand for our true values of liberty, equality, and diversity especially in these difficult times.

Sincerely,
